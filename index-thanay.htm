About IEDC IESCE

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at IES College of Engineering, Thrissur, is a student-driven initiative established under the guidance of the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) and officially recognized by the Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), Government of Kerala.

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant culture of innovation, entrepreneurial thinking, and problem-solving among students. We conduct ideation workshops, startup bootcamps, pre-incubation programs, and industry mentorship sessions to help students convert their ideas into viable startups. As a KSUM-affiliated cell, we provide access to funding opportunities, co-working spaces, and statewide startup networks.
